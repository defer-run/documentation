Start _queueing without plumbing_ with Defer's open-source [Scheduler](/platform/scheduler) and [Console](/platform/console) that enable:

- **Queued executions** to [receive webhooks, handle OpenAI rate limiting or Vercel timeouts](/guides/background-tasks).
- **Scheduled executions** to [schedule email campaigns or batch processing](/guides/scheduled-tasks).
- **Workflows** to perform [LLM-chaining or long-running transactions](/guides/workflows).

**Get started in under 5 minutes** and scale without any infrastructure work.
